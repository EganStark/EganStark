## About

I am a CSE graduate from **East West University**, with a major in **Data Science** and a research focus on **explainable artificial intelligence**. I care about systems that do more than produce an answer: they should expose useful evidence, respect their users, and hold up outside a notebook.

- Published **4 peer-reviewed conference papers** across explainable AI, computer vision, agricultural intelligence, and environmental forecasting
- First author of a Swin Transformer study on rare and endangered medicinal plant classification
- Building across the full path from model experimentation and analysis to databases, interfaces, deployment, and documentation
- Open to opportunities in **Data Science, Machine Learning, and AI Engineering**
